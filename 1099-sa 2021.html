    HSA FOR INDIVIDUALS

    Have you heard about this? 

    Check out the PayFlex Individual HSA and see how much you could save. You don’t want to miss this opportunity if:

    • You’re enrolled in a qualified high-deductible health plan (HDHP) and your employer doesn’t offer an HSA.
    • You’re self-employed and you’re enrolled in a qualified HDHP.
    • You’re age 55 or older and your spouse has an HSA. You could open your own HSA and contribute $1,000 each year.
    • You’re under age 26 and covered by your parent’s HDHP. If you’re not claimed as a dependent on their tax return, you may be eligible to open your own HSA.
    • You have an HSA, but you’d like to open an HSA with PayFlex and transfer your funds so your money is all in one place.
